
\subsection*{Information Sources}

Relevant articles were identified through a systematic search of PubMed, Web of Science, Embase, Scopus, PsycINFO, CENTRAL and Google Scholar during January 2025, with the assistance of a librarian. Included articles then had their bibliography's searched by the primary author (JGE) on 14th May 2025 to identify any potentional additional literature not uncovered in the primary search.
