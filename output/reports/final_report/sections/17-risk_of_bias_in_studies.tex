\subsubsection*{Case Series}
Seperately, 3 studies \cite{Elghali_2016, Karp_1991b, Lee_2007} were evaluated using the JBI Checklist for Case Series \cite{Moola_2020}.
Reporting quality was generally high across all JBI domains. All included case series fully reported clear inclusion criteria, standard condition measurement, valid id method, complete inclusion, clear demographic reporting, clear clinical info reporting, clear outcome followup reported, and appropriate statistical analysis. However, fewer studies reported consecutive inclusion, and clear site demographic reporting.
